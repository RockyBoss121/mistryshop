About Us

U-Survey conducts mystery shopping on behalf of its clients belonging to different segments through physical visits to specific locations and conducting surveys, helping brands and retailers realize the customer experience through their shoppers' eyes. U-Survey is a fascinating method of gauging customer experience. The industries interested in fulfilling their customer demands and maximization of their profits, U-Survey Mystery shoppers come and play a vital role in this by providing insights into the real on-ground situations. U-Survey helps the companies to identify the weak areas and making proper strategies to act upon them to ensure desired customer experience in their product segment. U-Survey also allows Corporations to test employee customer service training, assumptions, and CX initiatives through its Mystery shoppers.

U-Survey's Mystery Shopping Program identifies the performance of the business stream. Each program focuses on different topics: identifying improvement areas, selling, and benchmarking with the competition. U-Survey Mystery shoppers can provide services to numerous industries such as Mobile Network Operators, Banks, societies, communities, Dealers, Healthcare providers, Garment Retail chains, Automobiles, Luxury services, Movie theatres, Resorts, Restaurant chains, etc.
